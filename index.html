  • Conway's Soldiers

    Conway's Soldiers or the checker-jumping problem is a one-person mathematical game or puzzle devised and analyzed by mathematician John Horton Conway in 1961. The goal of the game is to move your soldiers up as far as you can. Soldiers can only move by jumping over another soldier into an empty square.
